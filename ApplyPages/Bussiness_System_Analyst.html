Business Systems Analyst

Apply Now

Job Description:

We are looking for an experienced Business Systems Analyst who will work with business stakeholders to identify, analyze, document, and refine detailed business needs of ranging complexity and scope. You will be responsible for designing functional solutions that appropriately meet business needs, reviewing test plans, and ensuring that test cases and results meet requirements.

Responsibilities:

  • ➽ Technical Delivery : Ability to administer and configure finance systems applications to support business processes while adhering to SOX-compliant change management.
  • ➽ Process Improvement : Accomplish improvements to finance processes that you support. Understanding the impacts of changes recommended and how to translate them into technical requirements. Document updated processes in the company handbook.
  • ➽ Business Acumen : Understanding of core finance business processes of quote to cash, record to report, and procure to pay. Understanding of accounting principles and finance-related policies. Motivation for continuous learning in the area.
  • ➽ Business Engagement : Engagement Engage with your team and stakeholders regarding their business strategy and objectives for automating and optimizing financial processes. Meet regularly with business partners to ensure that the financial applications are functioning as designed and supporting their needs.
  • ➽ Leadership : Ability to manage your work to deliver on agreed dates and deadlines and communicate any risks or issues to those.
  • ➽ Compliance : Supports data, security, and legal governance processes and adherence to guidelines between internal business units and systems in order to ensure efficiency and compliance with technical delivery.
  • ➽ Business Partnership:Support Systems administrators and Managers regarding business strategy, go-to-market, functional strategy, spending initiatives, ad hoc financial analysis, and monthly, quarterly, and annual planning. Maintain robust relationships with select cross-functional partners.
  • ➽ Project Management : Run small-sized projects that improve our ability to make better data-focused insights or make the company more efficient.
  • ➽ Autonomy : Accomplish goals with moderate supervision from senior members of the team.
  • ➽ Partner with management, business teams, and/or data team to implement solutions.

Minimum Experience:

  • ➽ 3-5 years of experience in a system administration role ideally with an enterprise SaaS software model.
  • ➽ BS degree in Computer Science, Cognitive Science, or relevant degree.
  • ➽ Demonstrated success working in cross-functional teams to meet business objectives and influence colleagues, fostering robust working relationships.
  • ➽ Ability for strategic thinking, with robust creative problem-solving and inquisitive skills.
  • ➽ Able to train application users on new tools and changes to existing tools.
  • ➽ Hands-on experience with business and financial software preferred. Salesforce, NetSuite, Zuora, and Coupa are a plus.
  • ➽ Familiarity with SOX ITGC Compliance.
  • ➽ Ability to thrive in a fully remote organization.
